OUR MISSION

At Solano Family Dentistry we believe in treating our patients like family. Our mission is to provide exceptional dental care for patients of all ages and backgrounds. We are committed to tailoring every treatment plan to fit the particular needs of each individual patient. We take great pride and responsibility in knowing that our patients have placed their trust in us, and are proud to offer the highest quality dental care in a friendly, professional, and comforting environment.

Our patients love our location near historic downtown Benicia. Our treatment room is filled with natural light, and boasts large windows overlooking a beautiful calming vista of the trees and water. We also offer plenty of parking on-site!

DR. SALEK

Dr. Mehdi Salek graduated from University of Illinois at Chicago College of Dentistry and completed his undergraduate degree in Biological Sciences at UCLA. He was also honored with a faculty appointment at the University of the Pacific Dental School in San Francisco where he taught clinical dentistry.

Dr. Salek enjoys working with patients of all ages. He is particularly skilled at helping patients who may be fearful or require extra gentle care. His warm and caring demeanor puts even the most fearful patients at ease. Most importantly, he loves helping his patients feel and look their best while maintaining their oral care and hygiene.
